# Audio Files

This folder contains background music and sound effects for the game.

## Adding Your Own Music

To add custom background music:

1. **Find royalty-free music** (or use your own):
   - [Pixabay Music](https://pixabay.com/music/) - Free music
   - [FreePD](https://freepd.com/) - Public domain music
   - [Incompetech](https://incompetech.com/) - Royalty-free music
   - [YouTube Audio Library](https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary)

2. **Download an ambient/horror music file**

3. **Convert to MP3** (if needed) and name it `ambient.mp3`

4. **Place the file** in this folder (`web/audio/ambient.mp3`)

5. **Refresh the game** - the music will play automatically!
